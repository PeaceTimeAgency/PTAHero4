Mission Briefing

Peace Time Agency operates at the nexus of digital media and interstellar diplomacy. Our mission is to leverage the power of TikTok LIVE to create authentic connections between civilizations, fostering understanding and cooperation across the galaxy.

Founded in 2087 amidst rising tensions in the Orion Arm, our agency has successfully mediated 47 major interstellar conflicts and facilitated cultural exchange programs reaching over 300 billion sentient beings.

Our Vision

A harmonious galaxy where cultural differences are celebrated and conflicts are resolved through dialogue and creative expression.

Our Method

Utilizing cutting-edge translation technology and immersive LIVE formats to create authentic cross-cultural experiences.
